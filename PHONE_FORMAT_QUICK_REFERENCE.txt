║                        VALIDATION RULES                                  ║
╚════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

✅ ACCEPTS:
   • 10-digit US numbers: 5551234567 → +15551234567
   • 11-digit with country code: 15551234567 → +15551234567
   • Already formatted: +15551234567 → +15551234567
   • Various formats: (555) 123-4567, 555-123-4567, etc.

❌ REJECTS:
   • Too few digits: 12345, 555123 (< 10 digits)
   • Too many digits: 111111111111111 (> 15 digits)
   • Repeated digits: 1111111111, 2222222222
   • Sequential: 1234567890, 0123456789
   • Invalid country codes: +211111111111
